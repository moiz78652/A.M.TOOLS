ABOUT A.M.TOOLS

Established in the year of 2001, We Am Tools is a leading and dynamic group of people acknowledged as manufacturer, wholesaler and trader of various industrial products under the segmented categories such as

IMPACT ADAPTERS, IMPACT SOCKET, SLOGGING SPANNER, ALLEN KEY, HOOK SPANNERS, INDUSTRIAL PIPE WRENCH, ETC.

These tools are synonyms to long life, high strength, sturdiness and cost-effectiveness. We believe in enhancing efficiency and performance of the products to which offered products are used as tools while giving high satisfaction to the clients for their worthy investment. They admire us for our focused customer endeavors and effective response to fulfill their requirements at the most earliest time period. Since its inception, the organization has been enhancing its competency and skills in relevant areas to develop products with high efficiency. Further, we have state-of-the-art infrastructure facility equipped with advance technology that further facilitates our skilled team of professionals in finishing their job with ease and perfection. Through large distribution network, we seek to remain on our commitment to deliver our products within predetermined time frame. Mr. Murtuza Abbas, our great mentor, has been driving us towards achieving newer heights in the business with an aim to become prominent and competent entity in the industry.
